Privacy Policy

Last updated: 08.02.26

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ProxySkriptsBot (“the Bot”) collects and uses data.

2. Data Collected

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server IDs
  • Channel IDs
  • Message content (only when required for commands)

3. Data Not Collected

  • Email addresses
  • Passwords
  • IP addresses
  • Payment information

4. Use of Data

Data is used only to operate and improve the Bot. Data is never sold or shared.

5. Data Storage

Most data is processed temporarily and not permanently stored. Stored data is protected against unauthorized access.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary and deleted afterward.

7. User Rights

Users may request access to or deletion of their data by contacting the developer.

8. Third-Party Services

The Bot operates on Discord and follows Discord’s Privacy Policy.

9. Children’s Information

The Bot is not intended for users under the age of 13.

10. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time.
